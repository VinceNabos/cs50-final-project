Overview

  • You have an identity. People you know have identities. Everyone has identities.
  • In the modern world, governments store citizens' informations in databases run by programs. Be it names, ages, or anything that is used to identify someone.
  • In this exercise, you will implement a program that displays a person's information.

Instructions

  • In Python, create a program that displays a person's information.
  • The program will take three inputs: name, age, and sex.
  • In this scenario, a person is identified by the following syntax: [NAME] [AGE][SEX]
  • Your program must print() the person's information to the command line!

Test Cases

  • You may assume that all names and sexes are strings.
  • You may assume that all ages are integers.
  • Your printed output must strictly follow the output syntax.
